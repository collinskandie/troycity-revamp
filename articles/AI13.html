Conclusions

As we have seen, creating email marketing campaigns can mark a before and after in the growth of your company, giving you the opportunity to attract new potential customers, track their behavior in different media, educate them, and even build loyalty so that they always come back, either for your service, product or what you can provide at any given time.

However, sometimes, choosing which email marketing software to use is not always an easy choice. On the internet, there is too much information of all kinds, as well as misinformation, so making the right decisions can be more difficult than usual.

To avoid making the wrong decision, you need to pay special attention to each of the features offered by each platform, but you also need to understand the functions you need in your business to be able to grow without failing in the attempt. Therefore, it is necessary to recognize if you understand what you need or if you should look for more information or educate yourself to have clear ideas when choosing an option.

Either way, we present you 10 options of email marketing tools, each one with acceptable functions to start testing and trying, but, depending on the characteristics and needs of your business, you may have to start deciding between one or the other.
