About Us

Troycity Africa is a professional consulting and solutions company dedicated to helping businesses, nonprofits, and institutions achieve growth, sustainability, and impact. We bring together expertise in market research, business strategy, financial consultancy, technology, and capacity building to deliver practical solutions that drive real results.

“Let’s start small. See some results. Once we see that this is working and we’ve made some money, we can look at stepping up to a bigger budget down the road”. -Nearly Every Client We’ve Ever Worked With We get it. Most of our clients started “smaller”.

We can develop a strategy that allows you to grow without causing you to spend more than you’re comfortable with. A great solution is one that helps you achieve your goals without causing you “budget stress”!

Our Mission

To empower organizations with innovative strategies, financial insights, and digital solutions that enable them to thrive in a competitive and ever-changing environment. We are committed to:

  • Driving Growth – By providing innovative business strategies, and market-driven solutions that enable sustainably.
  • Fostering Innovation – By digital transformation, and research to help clients lead in their industries.
  • Enhancing Capacity – Through tailored capacity-building initiatives that equip organizations with practical skills for long-term success.
  • Promoting Accountability – By ensuring financial transparency, compliance, and sound governance in all business operations.
  • Creating Lasting Impact – By designing solutions that contribute to community development and sustainable growth across the globe.

Our Vision

To be Africa’s trusted partner in business growth, innovation, and transformation, creating lasting value for communities and economies. We envision a continent where:

  • Businesses Flourish – Entrepreneurs and organizations have access to the right strategies, funding, and digital tools to scale and succeed.
  • Innovation Leads Progress – Technology drive solutions that solve Africa’s unique challenges and open global opportunities.
  • Communities Prosper – Business success goes hand-in-hand with social impact, empowering communities and improving livelihoods.
  • Sustainability Shapes Growth – Organizations embrace responsible, climate-conscious, and ethical practices that secure long-term prosperity.
  • Excellence Becomes the Standard – African businesses compete on a global stage through professionalism, innovation, and resilience.
